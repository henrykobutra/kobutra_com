THE THINGS I DO

Progress beats perfection

I will always be a lifelong learner. However, my 13 years of experience from startups to enterprises, from ecommerce to financial services, from starting as a software developer to managing large teams, from team meetings to board rooms, got distilled into the following skill categories

  • People Management (13 Years)

    The best way to manage people in my books is to make myself obsolete. The point where I consider myself successful as a people manager is when the team can continue my work without my presence.

  • Product Management (10 Years)

    IMHO: Empathy is the most important trait of a product manager. Caring deeply about customer problems and how to solve them. BUT, equally important is the ability to work well with designers and developers.

  • Product Analytics (10 Years)

    I have created mobile app and web analytics playbooks for almost 10 years now using tag managers, firebase, google analytics, appsflyer, and more.

  • Product Marketing (10 Years)

    Besides devloping Go-To-Market strategies, I specialize in performance marketing to deliver customer acquisition, engagement, and retention campaigns.
